My favourite group

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name.
The group is composed of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

Each member of group have their own position:

  • Bang Chan:Leader, Producer, Vocalist, Dancer, Rapper
  • Lee Know:Dancer, Vocalist, Rapper
  • Seo Changbin:Dancer, Vocalist, Rapper
  • Hwang Hyunjin:Dancer, Rapper, Vocalist, Visual
  • Han Jisung:Rapper,Vocalist,Dancer,Producer,Ace
  • Lee Felix:Rapper,Dancer,Vocalist
  • Kim Seungmin:Vocalist
  • I.N. :Vocalist,Maknae
